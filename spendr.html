Spendr – Personal Finance App

UI/UX Case Study: Budgeting & Expense Tracking

Spendr is a personal UI/UX design project I created to help users take control of their finances. The app focuses on budgeting, expense tracking, and goal monitoring, with a clean and intuitive interface. I designed user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity mockups to demonstrate how users can visualize their spending and make informed decisions.
